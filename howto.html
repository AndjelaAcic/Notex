Global Community of Students

Revolutionary blockchain-based app that allows students to easily upload and download notes from their peers by exchanging Notex coins. Notex is a platform designed for students by students, with the aim of facilitating the sharing of knowledge and making education accessible to all.

Notex offers a range of benefits to students, including the ability to access high-quality notes on a wide variety of subjects. By using Notex, students can quickly and easily find notes that are relevant to their coursework, saving them time and effort in their studies. In addition, Notex allows students to connect with others in their field of study, creating a sense of community and fostering collaboration.

Notex also offers a number of other benefits, including the ability to earn Notex coins through activities such as creating high-quality notes, participating in community discussions, and referring others to the platform. This means that students can not only benefit from the platform themselves but can also earn rewards for contributing to the community.

Your files are secured by Blockchain technology

One of the most unique features of Notex is its use of blockchain technology. This means that all transactions on the platform are transparent and secure, with users able to trust in the integrity of the system. By using Notex coins, students can easily exchange value with one another, making it a simple and efficient way to compensate others for their work.

Decentralization is a key feature of many modern services, and it's no different for Notex. By using blockchain technology, Notex is able to offer users a fully decentralized platform that operates independently of any central authority. This means that users benefit from a high degree of transparency, security, and reliability, as all transactions on the platform are verified by a network of users rather than a single entity.

All files are stored on the blockhain using IPFS protocol so you will never need to worry about your intellectual property being stored on an centralized server.
